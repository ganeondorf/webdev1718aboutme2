The Juicy Details

Art has been part of my life since birth. Many times you could find me trying to draw something that I saw on tv or in life. Obviously I couldn't draw very well so I would beg my father or my cousin to draw me something cool. Eventually I got sick and tired of it, I needed to be able to create my own things. So I set out on a mission, draw as much as I can. And in the end I feel as though I've grown exponentially in my trial and error.
